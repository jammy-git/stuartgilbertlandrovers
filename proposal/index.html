About us

Stuart and David Gilbert. A father and son workshop.

For more than seventy-five years between us, we've specialised in one thing. Keeping Land Rovers running properly for owners across Kent. We know these vehicles because we've spent our working lives under them.

You'll speak to Stuart or David when you call. Same pair of hands, every visit. No call centres, no service managers. It's just the two of us, the workshop and a lot of Land Rover parts.
